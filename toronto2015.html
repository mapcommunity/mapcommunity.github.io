Workshop on Algebra, Geometry and Proofs in Symbolic Computation

December 7 - 16, 2015 (Toronto)

Co-organizers:

  • Georges Gonthier (Microsoft Research)
  • Marie-Francoise Roy (University of Rennes I)
  • Eric Schost (University of Western Ontario)

Program Committee:
Saugata Basu, James Davenport, Jean-Charles Faugere, Georges Gonthier, Peter Paule, Marie-Francoise Roy, Eric Schost

This workshop was celebrated in the framework of the Thematic Program on Computer Algebra, which was held in Toronto from July to December of 2015.
