Monastir, 2009

The annual meeting of the MAP community, in this edition, was held in Monastir (Tunisia). It supposed a continuation of the first seminar "Verification and Constructive Algebra" (Dagstuhl, January 2003).

A poster presenting the event is available in english and french.

The Conference consisted of two main tutorials together with talks of the participants focused on the topics of the MAP group. The tutorials were:

  • Formalization of Finite Groups Theory (G. Gonthier)
  • Formalization of Algebraic Topology (J. Rubio)
The complete program of the Conference and the list of participants can be found in the following pdf file.
